\item An alternative to land conveyances by deed is the Torrens title system, in
which the government's recordation system is conclusive evidence of ownership.
In other words, in a Torrens title jurisdiction, one conveys land not by
executing and delivering a deed, but by recording the conveyance with the
government. What are the advantages and disadvantages of this system, with
respect to buyers, sellers, and the public?
